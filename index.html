Hello new world!

Uploading my dissertation to ProQuest, a monumental moment, was followed by a night of lesson planning and grading. Like any teacher USA, Sunday nights are a mental parkour with emotions percolating faster than the morning coffee. First- period-Monday-morning-high-schoolers need their teacher to BRING IT or stay home. While it seems as if nothing has changed, in fact, there is a whole new world I get to explore as Dr. Lee. The path is foggy at the moment, but each day brings new opportunities and possibilities. All I have to do is take another step.
